Is exporting Vlogs free?

Your first export is free. After that, a one-time ¥490 purchase (“Vlog Export”) unlocks unlimited exports. No subscriptions, no extra charges, no ads.

Where are my videos stored?

Entirely on your iPhone. There is no developer server, and your footage is never uploaded. Exported Vlogs are added to Photos only when you tap “Save to Camera Roll”.

How do I use it with travel companions?

From the trip’s Album tab, tap “Invite companions” to send a link, or show the QR code to join on the spot in seconds. You can edit the itinerary and packing list together. Both iPhones need to be signed in to iCloud.

Can I share with Android friends?

Sharing currently works between iPhones (Apple IDs) only.

What about music copyright?

Built-in tracks are by Kevin MacLeod (CC BY 4.0). When you share an exported Vlog, a credit line is added to the caption automatically. For music you import yourself, please check the rights on your own.

How do I delete my data completely?

Deleting a trip also removes its data from iCloud. Deleting the App erases everything on your device; anything left in iCloud can be managed from Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. See the Privacy Policy for details.
