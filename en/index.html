FAQ

Frequently asked

Start here if something's not working

QIs exporting a Vlog free?

Your first export is free. From the second one, a one-time ¥490 "Vlog Export" unlocks unlimited exports. No subscriptions, no extra charges, no ads.

QWhere are my videos stored?

Entirely on your iPhone. There is no developer server, and your footage is never uploaded. Exported Vlogs are added to Photos only when you tap "Save to Camera Roll".

QHow do I use it with companions?

From the trip's Album tab, tap "Invite companions" to share a link, or show the QR code to join on the spot in seconds. You can edit the itinerary and packing list together; both iPhones need to be signed in to iCloud.

QCan I use it with Android friends?

Sharing currently works between iPhones (Apple IDs) only.

QI'm worried about music licensing

The built-in BGM comes from Pixabay under the Pixabay Content License (commercial use allowed, no attribution required). You can include it in exported videos as-is. For music you import yourself, please check the rights on your own.

QI want to delete everything

Deleting a trip also removes its data from iCloud. Deleting the app erases everything on your device; anything left in iCloud can be managed from iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. See the Privacy Policy for details.
